Who We Are

Founded in 1996, Charles & Keith is a Singapore-based fashion brand that has grown into a global name synonymous with contemporary style and accessible luxury.

What began as a single boutique has blossomed into an international brand with stores across Asia, Europe, and the Middle East. Our journey has been guided by a commitment to innovation, quality craftsmanship, and empowering our customers to express their individuality through fashion.

Our Design Philosophy

At Charles & Keith, we believe luxury should be accessible without compromising on quality or design. Our creative team draws inspiration from global trends, architectural forms, and the dynamic energy of modern cities.

Each collection is thoughtfully crafted to combine style with practicality, ensuring our customers look and feel their best in every situation.

Quality Craftsmanship

We meticulously source materials and employ skilled artisans to create products that stand the test of time.

Sustainable Practices

Committed to reducing our environmental impact through responsible sourcing and production methods.

Customer Focus

Your satisfaction is our priority. We strive to exceed expectations at every touchpoint.
